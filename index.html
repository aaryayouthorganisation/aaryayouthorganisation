About Us

Aarya Youth Organization, registered under the Societies Act of XXI of 1860 (RS/CA/243/R/16 of 2023-24), is a dynamic NGO dedicated to youth empowerment, cultural enrichment, and sports excellence. Our mission is to build resilient communities by providing educational resources, celebrating cultural diversity, and promoting inclusive sports opportunities. With a focus on transparency and ethical practices, Aarya Youth Organization actively engages with communities to create a positive impact and foster a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
